Our Practice Areas

  1. Dispute Resolution and Arbitration

    Our firm has a robust and active Dispute Resolution Team with expertise in assisting our clients in civil, criminal commercial and constitutional disputes in various forums. Apart from litigation, our firm has a strong presence in conducting domestic and international arbitration, mediation and conciliation across diverse sectors.

    Initium Law’s Dispute Resolution and Arbitration team provides a full spectrum of services including the following -

    • Assistance in Civil, Commercial and Criminal Litigation by drafting and representing clients in various courts
    • Constitutional and writ litigation in High Court and Supreme Court
    • Drafting of Arbitration Agreements and conducting commercial arbitration
    • Conducting domestic and international mediation and conciliation
    • Consumer Protection and Product Liability
    • Intellectual Property infringement

  2. Intellectual Property Law

    Our firm has a dedicated team of IPR attorneys who offer comprehensive range of services including the process of creating, protecting and enforcing the IP rights of our clients. Our team has experience in advising clients ranging from startups to MNC’s in all aspects of Intellectual Property including Patent, Trademark, Copyright and Industrial Design. Our team’s specialized expertise include -

    • Assistance in Patent, trademark and copyright registration
    • Drafting of Licensing agreements and creating franchising models
    • Conducting IP due diligence
    • IP litigation and dispute resolution
    • Trade secret protection and disputes
    • Opposition and Rectification

  3. Insolvency & Restructuring

    We provide a holistic range of services which encompasses both litigation and the non-litigation work in the sphere of Insolvency & Restructuring. From ascertaining early signs of distress in a company and assisting in their revival to assisting the stakeholders in Corporate Resolution Process and in liquidation of the company, our practice is all encompassing. A glimpse of the scope of services we provide are given below -

    • Advisory on Individual and Corporate Bankruptcy
    • Negotiations with creditors for settlements and restructuring
    • Represent various stakeholders including Financial Creditor, Corporate Debtor and CoC, IRP and RP before NCLT, NCLAT, High Courts and Supreme Court of India.
    • Advisory on Liquidation and Voluntary Liquidations.

  4. Corporate and Commercial Advisory

    Our Corporate and Commercial Advisory team is involved with our clients in all stages of a corporate entity, right from the inception of a company, its day to day management, regulatory affairs, to the dissolution of the company. As per the economic activities undertaken by our clients, our advice enables our clients to avoid or mitigate risks. Our Corporate and Commercial Practice includes the following -

    • Incorporation and formation of companies
    • Corporate governance and compliance
    • Drafting of Shareholder agreements and resolving related disputes
    • Mergers and Acquisitions
    • Conducting Due diligence
    • Obtaining government approvals and clearances

  5. Banking and Finance

    Our Team offers a complete range of services in the area of Banking and Finance in order to meet the requirements of all stakeholders in a financing transaction. We assist our clients for the entire life cycle of a transaction ranging from the initial structuring to closing including regulatory compliance, drafting of transactional documents and due diligence. Our range of services include -

    • Advisory on financial regulations and compliance
    • Structuring and negotiating loans and other financial agreements
    • Advisory on debt acquisition through Asset Reconstruction Company or NBFCs
    • Enforcement of Security Interest under SARFAESI Act
    • Securities and derivatives transactions.
    • Drafting of term sheets and transactional documents
    • Debt and equity financing.
    • Investment fund formation and management.

  6. Start Up Legal Compliance

    We understand that running a startup is a challenging task for the founders. To enable the startup to achieve its end goal and ensure its smooth functioning, we provide a one stop solution by assisting the startup in all stages of its development from business formation, statutory compliance, protection of intellectual property, general advisory related to issues in day to day management of the entity and timely exit strategies. Our team provides a comprehensive range of services including -

    • Providing end to end services including incorporation services, corporate compliance and labour practices
    • Drafting, negotiating and vetting of contracts
    • Filing of statutory compliances
    • Designing exit strategies
    • Drafting website policies

    • Divorce, Child Custody, and Support
    • Adoption and guardianship
    • Prenuptial agreements
    • Domestic violence and restraining orders
    • Property division and alimony

  8. Employment Law

Our clientele is diverse and includes employees, board of directors and HR personnel.
